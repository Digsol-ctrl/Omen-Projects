About Omen Projects

Professional construction, architectural design, project management and bills of quantities — Bulawayo-based. Your trusted construction partner for projects managed from abroad. We handle the technical delivery while you oversee from wherever you are.

Who We Are

Omen Projects is a growing but professional construction firm headquartered in Bulawayo, Zimbabwe. We specialize in serving the Zimbabwean diaspora — providing transparent, professionally managed construction delivery for clients building from abroad. We combine practical construction experience with strong project governance to deliver high-quality buildings on time and within budget.

Our core services include building construction, architectural design, project management and bills of quantities (BOQ).

Experience: Years of hands-on expertise across residential, commercial and renovation projects — structured to scale while maintaining professional delivery.

What Makes Us Different

  • Structured project management: Clear stages, milestones and reporting so projects run predictably.
  • Clear communication: Regular updates and documented decisions — especially important for diaspora clients.
  • Emphasis on quality & compliance: We follow industry best practices to meet safety and regulatory requirements.
  • Local delivery, diaspora-friendly: Remote approvals, transparent cost breakdowns (BOQs) and reliable supply-chain arrangements.

Our Approach

We combine technical expertise with practical site management, clear procurement processes and consistent client communication. Typical delivery includes scoping, design coordination, BOQ preparation, procurement, construction and handover with documented warranties.

For Diaspora Clients

Building back home from anywhere in the world.

We provide complete remote project oversight so you can confidently build in Zimbabwe while living abroad. Our services include:

  • Regular video updates and photo documentation
  • Clear cost breakdowns (BOQs) transparent pricing
  • Remote approval workflows
  • Trusted local suppliers and skilled labour
  • Professional compliance with Zimbabwean regulations
